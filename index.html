Austin Open Source GIS

Austin Open Source GIS (atx-osg) is for people interested in learning about, using, developing, or promoting open source tools in the field of Geographic Information Systems. Members of atx-osg are GIS professionals, developers and students passionate about generating more open source discussion in the local geospatial community. Membership is free and open to all.
